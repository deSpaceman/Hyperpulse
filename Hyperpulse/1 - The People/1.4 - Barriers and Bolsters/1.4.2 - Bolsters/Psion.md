### Psion
__Type:__ Bolster

Either by genetic luck, interactions with the weird of the galaxy, or just because some forgotten trickster Veil Entity thought it would be funny, you have psychic powers.

You may cast Telekinesis, with no cost, an amount of times equals to your Mind divided by two per day, rounded down, to a minimum of 1. Follow the description and Keywords found on the spell as if you were casting it normally.

__Effect Value:__ Major
